Six Wishes, One Love

🌸 — I —

For Your Endless Warmth

You are the golden light that fills every room you enter — a warmth that has held us through every storm and celebrated every sunrise. Today, we return that light to you.

🌷 — II —

For Your Quiet Strength

The world may never know the weight you carried in silence — the sleepless nights, the sacrifices unseen. But we know. And we are forever grateful for the grace with which you bore it all.

🌹 — III —

For Your Boundless Joy

Your laughter is the melody our family dances to. May this day bring you the same radiant happiness you've given us a thousand times over — you deserve every beautiful thing.

🌺 — IV —

For Your Gentle Wisdom

Every piece of advice, every knowing glance, every "I told you so" delivered with love — your wisdom has been our compass. Thank you for always pointing us toward the light.

💐 — V —

For Your Unconditional Love

You loved us before we knew how to love ourselves. Through every phase, every mistake, every triumph — your love remained the one constant we could always come home to.

🌻 — VI —

For Simply Being You

Not every hero wears a cape. Some wear aprons, carry purses full of tissues, and have the supernatural ability to make everything better with a single hug. You are our hero.

A Letter Written in Love
She was the first voice we ever heard, and the first heart we ever felt beat.

Dear Mother,

There are words that exist in no language — the ones that live in the space between a mother's heartbeat and her child's first breath. You carried us in that space, nurtured us in that silence, and loved us before we had a name.

You taught us that tenderness is not weakness, that patience is not passivity, and that the strongest force in the universe is a mother's love — quiet, fierce, and absolutely unbreakable.

Every flower that blooms remembers the soil that held it. Every star that shines remembers the darkness that cradled it. And every one of us, in every moment of our lives, remembers you.

You are not just our mother. You are the reason we believe in love at all.
With all the love we hold,
— Your Children
